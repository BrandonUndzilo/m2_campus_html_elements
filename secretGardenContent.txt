Page Description:
The Secret Garden is a family-owned garden supply center located in Bellevue, Washington. We sell everything you need to build the perfect yard or garden, and our experts are here to help!

H2 - Welcome to the Secret Garden!
Text (below hero image):
At The Secret Garden, our passion for plants has helped us to become the largest garden center in the state! We are a family-owned business, founded in 1967, that has served our friends and neighbors in the Seattle area with pride since opening. As we have grown over the years, we’ve focused on offering the healthy, beautiful, and Pacific-Northwest-friendly plants to our friends and neighbors. We grow many of our plants from seed, and are always working to ensure that our custom plant varieties are hardy and beautiful. We are open seven days a week at our location at 1234 Main Street in Bellevue, come visit us today!

H2 - Plants at The Secret Garden
Text below heading (also in description):
The Secret Garden in Bellevue, W.A. offers annuals, perennials, succulents, and more! Our outdoor plants are made for Washington weather, and we are here to help you find everything you need. Learn more about our plants before you buy!

Annuals (heading next to icon and above the annual plant images) 
Icon: tree.png - white tree outline graphic
plants that fall in the annuals category must be planted or replaced each year. They will not survive harsh winter weather, but bring beautiful color to your garden in spring, summer, or fall!

Perennials (heading next to the icon and above the perennial plant images) 
Icon: flower.png - white flower outline graphic with six petals
perennials are plants that do not need to be replaced or planted each year. Perennials can be used to add structure to your yard or garden and will grow in size from year to year.
